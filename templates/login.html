• The Spotipyn Project

  • Spotipyn is a web-based application built using Spotipy that allows users to add annotations to songs.
  • These annotations, or 'pins', are able to be freely moved around and can have text attached to them.
  • Our repository is open-source and able to be edited from our GitHub.
  • To start, click the login button at the top right corner to authenticate with your Spotify account!
